Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This is your target score.

There are four crystals below (green, purple, blue, yellow). Each crystal has a specific number of points associated with it. When you click a crystal, that number of points will be added to your score.

You won't be told how many points each crystal is worth; you have to figure that out on your own.

The goal of the game is to make your score equal to the target score. If you achieve this, you win! If you go over the target score, you lose.

The target score and the value associated with each crystal will change every game.
